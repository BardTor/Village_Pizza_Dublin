About

We make 48-hour, fermented, wood fired Neapolitan style pizzas. We like to keep the pies close to the fire, which combined with the long proofing time, gives our pizzas plenty of beautiful charr. The slight bitter note of the charr compliments the sweetness and acidity of the tomatoes and creaminess of the mozzarella so well
We aim to use fresh and seasonal, Irish grown produce as much as possible. We even grow some of it ourselves.
